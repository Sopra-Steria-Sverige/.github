# Sopra Steria Sweden 🇸🇪

We're the Swedish arm of Sopra Steria, a European technology consultancy with around 50,000 people across 30 countries. In Sweden, we are in the top 5 of "Great place to work" and have around 600 consultants across offices in Stockholm, Göteborg, Malmö and Östersund. We're growing, particularly in AI, Cloud, Open source, data sovereignty and everything security and data privacy.

We work with across multiple industries, from startups to government agencies and everything in between. But we specialize mostly on customers who needs systems that need to run reliably for years and needs to handle multiple types of events. A few examples of the kind of work we do:

- 🏛️ **Public sector** — long-running engagements with Swedish myndigheter on case management systems, digital services for citizens, and migrations off legacy mainframe platforms. This includes work with agencies in social insurance, tax, transport, and healthcare domains.
- 🏦 **Banking and finance** — core system modernization, payment infrastructure, and regulatory reporting (PSD2, DORA, AML) for Nordic banks and insurers.
- ⚡ **Energy and industry** — operational systems for utilities and manufacturers, including SCADA integration, asset management, and the data platforms underneath them.
- 🛡️ **Defense and security** — cleared engagements supporting Defense tech, Nordic Defense, Swedish total defense and critical infrastructure clients, with the staffing and process requirements that implies.

Typical outcomes our clients measure us on: reducing time-to-production for new services from months to weeks, cutting infrastructure cost after cloud migration, meeting regulatory deadlines without disrupting existing operations, and keeping critical systems running through multi-year modernization programmes.

## What we do

- **Software engineering** — building and modernizing business-critical applications, often in regulated environments
- **Cloud and platform engineering** — designing and operating infrastructure on AWS, Azure, and GCP
- **Data and AI** — data platforms, ML systems, and applied AI integrated into existing operations
- **Cybersecurity** — assessment, implementation, and managed services
- **Systems integration** — connecting legacy and modern systems where downtime isn't an option
