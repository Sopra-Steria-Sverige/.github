# Sopra Steria Sweden 🇸🇪

> Vi bygger det som måste fungera.

We're the Swedish arm of Sopra Steria, a European technology consultancy with around 50,000 people across 30 countries. In Sweden, we have around 600 consultants across offices in Stockholm, Göteborg, Malmö, and Östersund.  
We're in the top 5 of "Great Place to Work®", and we're growing. Particularly in AI, cloud, open source, data sovereignty, and everything security and data privacy.

We work across multiple industries, from startups to government agencies and everything in between, but we specialize in customers who need systems that run reliably for years and handle a wide range of events. A few examples of the kind of work we do:

-  **Public sector**: Long-running engagements with Swedish myndigheter on case management systems, digital services for citizens, and migrations off legacy mainframe platforms. This includes work with agencies in social insurance, tax, transport, and healthcare domains.
-  **Banking and finance**: Core system modernization, payment infrastructure, and regulatory reporting for Nordic banks and insurers.
-  **Energy and industry**: Operational systems for utilities and manufacturers, including SCADA integration, asset management, and the data platforms underneath them.
-  **Defence and security**: Cleared engagements supporting defence tech, Nordic defence, Swedish total defence, and critical infrastructure clients, with the staffing and process requirements that implies.

## What we do

- **Software engineering**: Building and modernizing business-critical applications, often in regulated environments
- **Cloud and platform engineering**: Designing and operating infrastructure mainly on AWS and Azure, but also on GCP, Oracle, and European cloud and infrastructure providers — including our own data centres
- **Data and AI**: Data platforms, ML systems, and applied AI integrated into existing operations
- **Cybersecurity**: Assessment, implementation, and managed services
- **Systems integration**: Connecting legacy and modern systems where downtime isn't an option

## About this GitHub organization

This is where Sopra Steria Sweden publishes work we think is useful outside our client engagements:

- Open source libraries and tools built or maintained by our engineers
- Reference implementations and example code from talks, workshops, and articles
- Contributions to projects we depend on

Client code stays with our clients. Nothing here is confidential or proprietary to a customer.
